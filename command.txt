To copy/ download the File: 
  Powershell: curl "http://shakespeare.mit.edu/cymbeline/full.html" -O "data.txt"
To count the number of times speaker spoke and put it to a file  
  Bash:  grep -c "Queen" data.txt > resultSpeaker_1.txt
To count the number of times speaker 1 spoke and put it to a file:
  Bash:  grep -c "CYMBELINE" data.txt > resultSpeaker_2.txt
